Understanding Our Metrics


Explanation for Cost Reduction:


Lower Energy Consumption:
Since power consumption is one of the biggest cost drivers, reducing it leads to lower electricity bills.

Efficiency Gains:
Assuming you're optimizing production processes, using energy-efficient machinery, or reducing waste, you should expect a gradual decline in costs.
Non-Linear Cost Reduction:

Notice how the optimized bill declines faster in later months. This could indicate:
Delayed impact of energy-saving measures.
Compounding benefits of optimizations.
